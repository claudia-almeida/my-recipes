Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

This chicken noodle soup is cozy, simple, and perfect when you want something warm and nourishing. It’s made with tender chicken, classic vegetables, and noodles simmered in a flavorful broth.

It’s comforting, easy to customize, and tastes even better the next day. Serve it with crackers or crusty bread for the ultimate comfort meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast (or shredded rotisserie chicken)
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups egg noodles
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large pot, bring chicken broth to a gentle boil.
  2. Add carrots, celery, and onion, then simmer for 8–10 minutes until slightly softened.
  3. Add chicken and cook until fully cooked through (or add pre-cooked shredded chicken).
  4. Stir in egg noodles and simmer until noodles are tender, about 6–8 minutes.
  5. Add garlic, salt, and pepper, then simmer for 1–2 more minutes.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve hot.
