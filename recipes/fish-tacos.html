Fish Tacos

Description

These fresh and flavorful fish tacos are light, zesty, and perfect for a quick weeknight dinner. Tender white fish is seasoned with warm spices and pan-seared until flaky, then tucked into warm corn tortillas.

Topped with crisp cabbage slaw, a squeeze of lime, and a creamy sauce, these tacos strike the perfect balance of texture and flavor in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb white fish fillets (such as cod or tilapia)
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Season the fish with chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the fish for 3â€“4 minutes per side until flaky.
  3. Warm the corn tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  4. Mix sour cream and garlic to create a simple sauce.
  5. Assemble the tacos with fish, shredded cabbage, and sauce.
  6. Squeeze fresh lime juice over the top before serving.
