Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is layered with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella cheese. It’s a comforting, crowd-pleasing dish perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

The layers bake together into a golden, bubbling masterpiece that tastes even better the next day.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown the ground beef and mix with marinara sauce.
  4. In a bowl, combine ricotta, egg, salt, and pepper.
  5. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  6. Repeat layers and top with Parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake for 30–35 minutes until bubbly and golden.
