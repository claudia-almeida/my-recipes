Turkey Burger

Description

This juicy turkey burger is a lighter alternative to traditional beef burgers without sacrificing flavor. Made with lean ground turkey and simple seasonings, it’s tender, savory, and perfect for grilling or pan-searing.

Serve it on a toasted bun with fresh lettuce, tomato, and your favorite toppings for a satisfying and wholesome meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, and condiments of choice

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix ground turkey, breadcrumbs, egg, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Form the mixture into four equal patties.
  3. Heat a skillet or grill over medium heat.
  4. Cook patties for 5–6 minutes per side until fully cooked (internal temp 165°F).
  5. Toast burger buns if desired.
  6. Assemble burgers with lettuce, tomato, and your favorite toppings.
